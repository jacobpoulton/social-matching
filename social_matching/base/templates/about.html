About

Information about the project!

  1. You fill out a short personality form.
  2. We'll match you with other people based on your personality.
  3. You play a game(s) of Among Us with your new group members.
  4. Hopefully you can make new friendships, or at least have a good time!

The purpose of this study is to investigate matching people together to play games, and reduce their social isolation. In particular, we're looking at the affect of a game which is 'casual', 'approachable' and ' easily available', such as the chosen game for this study, Among Us.

Among Us is a social deduction game. Each round a few randomly chosen players try to sabotage everyone else, who have to find the impostors' identities before it's too late.
